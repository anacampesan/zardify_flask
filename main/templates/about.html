About

I'm Ana, also known as anazard by close friends, a music and technology enthusiast from Brazil. I've spent 18 months from 2014 to 2015 in Toronto, Canada.

Studying programming and being interested in technology since I was 5 years old got me into Systems Analysis and Development, which I'll be graduating from very soon.

At the same time, I spent many hours of my childhood playing guitar and practicing with some friends in garage bands. Within the years, my musical taste drastically changed from hard rock to indie, synth-based and experimental. As it got sort of hard to find people willing to play these things, I dug into home recording (the results you can check on my soundcloud).

Something I've ever wanted to do is building my own website. I did some experiences in the old Front Page times over 10 years ago. I had also built paralel stuff on subjects I used to like. However, my first dynamic webpage was developed in 2012, that is when I started learning server-side programming for an assignment at university. This website is basically the successful attempt of creating something fun for both me and the readers.

Things I do: back-end web development in Python (Flask), PHP and AJAX; research on Human-Computer Interaction, Software Engineer and Virtual Reality.
